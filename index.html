Download facebook videos online

To download video from facebook online using Facebook's website on your computer:

  1. Play the video you wish to download.
  2. Right-click and select show video url
  3. Copy and paste the video URL in the input above to download.

How to download videos from facebook to iPhone

As of today, you can't download a video from facebook to iPhone directly without using an app, what you can do instead is toget a direct link to the video and share on whatsapp. To do that using facebook app on iPhone:

  1. Tap and play the video you wish to download.
  2. While in full screen mode, tap share at the bottom-right corner.
  3. Select copy link and paste in the input above to download.

How to download videos from facebook on Android

To download a video from facebook on android the process is simple.On your android phone, open the facebook app and find the video you wish to download:

  1. Tap the post menu at the top-right corner.
  2. Scroll down and select copy link
  3. Copy and paste the video URL in the input above to download.
