How to download videos from Facebook

Download videos from facebook directly to your android device, iphone or pc in a simple and intuitive way with this free interactive tool.

To get started, copy and paste the URL of the video you wish to download from facebook in the designated box above and the video will start downloading automatically.

Download videos from facebook on iPhone:

To get a link to a video using facebook app on iPhone:

  1. Play the video you wish to download.
  2. Tap share at the bottom-right corner.
  3. Select copy link and paste in the box above to download.

Download videos from facebook on Android:

To get a link to a video using facebook app on Android:

  1. Tap the post menu at the top-right corner.
  2. Scroll down and select copy link
  3. Copy and paste the video URL in the box above to download.

Download videos from facebook on desktop website:

To get a link to a video using facebook desktop site on the web:

  1. Play the video you wish to download.
  2. Right-click and select show video url
  3. Copy and paste the video URL in the box above to download.
